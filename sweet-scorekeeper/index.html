甜甜计分(sweet scorekeeper)

感谢你对我的应用有兴趣!
Thank you for your interest in my App.

介绍(intro)

这是一个关于计分类的工具类应用。
通过这个应用，您可以在扑克、麻将等等回合制的计分游戏过程中实时的记录每轮的成绩，并自动进行汇总。此外，每次记录将会保存，方便您后续进行回顾和统计分析。
This is an excellent scores keeping app when playing poker, mahjong or other table games with your friends!
It provides an easy way to keep track of scores for 1 or more friends and accumulate the scores of everyone automatically.
Furthermore, each game result will be saved that you can review it later.

功能(features)

  • 朋友管理：支持添加和删除朋友，修改名字和头像
  • 开始游戏：支持选择任意数量的朋友开始游戏，游戏中可以对任意某轮的结果进行修改
  • 汇总统计：每局游戏的统计结果包括游戏时间以及每人总成绩和详细成绩，对了，首页上方的柱状图则显示你自己在过去12个月中每月总成绩
  • Friends：add and delete your friends，change his/her name, profile photo.
  • Gaming：you can select any number of friends to start game. also, you can jump back to any round to modify scores.
  • Stats：until now, the statistics for each game include time played and total score per person. in addition, the bar chart on the top of home page shows that monthly total score over the path 12 months.
